Moving from religious effort to the finished work of Christ.

Theme: Cast Out Religion

"Therefore if any person is [ingrafted] in Christ... the old has passed away. Behold, the fresh and new has come!" — 2 Cor 5:17

What religion did was to capture our consciousness with trying to be instead of the being finished work...

We no longer give to get, we get for it is given and it is ours to take. We give because our Father is a giver.

Golden Nugget: Christ Jesus in us, the hope of Glory.
