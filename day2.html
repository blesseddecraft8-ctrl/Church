Day 2 | 60 Days of Soul Mastery

The Benefits of Walking with God

14th March 2026

Theme Scripture: John 14:16-18
"I will not leave you orphans; I will come to you." (v.18)

Good morning. Today let us indulge in the walk with God. From the scriptures, we see the sons of Israel walking from Exodus with Him as in the appearance of a cloud at day and a pillar of fire at night.

They cross the sea with victory and journey on with constant comfort. Today, the Lord calls on you to walk with Him—allowing yourself to acknowledge Him as you journey through work, ministry, and all the work your hands touch. This comes with privileges that can't fit in a short devotion!

"It is a choice... You have to choose today to walk with Him and see the goodness of the Lord."

He says, "I am the Lord that heals you; you are to walk far away from fear." You cannot miss your right path if you avail yourself of the truth behind the decision of walking with Him daily.

Golden Nugget

You cannot miss your right way if you walk with God.

Further Study

  • Exodus 15
  • Mark 13:11
  • Galatians 2:20
  • John 14:6

Morning Prayer

Thank you God for this word. I have learned that you desire that I walk with you, and that I can only exist right if I walk with you. Today I decide to take further initiative in my Christian life to walk with you. Amen.
