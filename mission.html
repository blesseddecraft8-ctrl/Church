Our Mission

At HCIDM, our mission is to liberate the consciousness of believers from the chains of religious tradition and performance-based living. We aim to ground every heart in the finished work of Christ, moving from "doing to deserve" to "being in abundance."

The Vision

To see a generation of believers who live as true citizens of Heaven—empowered by grace, rooted in identity, and walking in the fullness of the New Creation.

"He has Risen, We Live"
