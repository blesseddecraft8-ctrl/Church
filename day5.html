Day 26 | Cast Out Religion

We Do Not Do to Deserve

Embracing the Finished Work of the Cross

Theme Scripture: 2 Cor. 5:17 (AMP) "Therefore if any person is [ingrafted] in Christ (the Messiah) he is a new creation (a new creature altogether); the old [previous moral and spiritual condition] has passed away. Behold, the fresh and new has come!"

The Consciousness of Being

Good morning child of God. What religion did was to capture our consciousness with trying to be instead of the being finished work. It has shown us the struggle to be like God but without Him.

From the cross, we are within Him. Casting away religion is changing our consciousness. They have taught us that He sits afar, so annoyed with man that we are worthless of His favor. Friend, when we read the word we read about us—His spirit is in us!

Selah.

We no longer give to get; we get for it is given and it is ours to receive. We don't give to help because we need from Him, but we give because our Father is a giver, and He fathered us in His own likeness. We give because we live in abundance.

Golden Nugget: What more shall we do except that which he endured for once down that road to Golgotha, with the stripes, with the nails. He died for once. And that is Christ Jesus in us the hope of Glory.

Prayer

Thank you Father for I am in you and you are in me. Thank you for I am bigger than what this world will ever have to explain. Father, I yearn to know you more for it's then that I may know me. Amen.
