Knowledge of Truth

13th March 2026

Theme Scripture: 1 Tim. 2:4

"Who desires all men to be saved and to come to the knowledge of the truth." (NKJV)

Good morning. Knowledge is necessary information passed on from one person or generation to another in preparation for a future. Just as in our engineering schools where we were equipped with knowledge to prepare for what lies ahead, divine knowledge is an exchange.

It is the renewal of our minds (Ephesians 4:23)—exchanging the wrong knowledge of a past life of sin for the truth God gives. Many Christians haven't advanced in growth because they didn't allow this equipping and transfer.

Even if you don't take the pulpit as a pastor or apostle, our mandate is to present the world to this truth through prayer, intercessions, and loving those who join the body more than the world ever could (1 Cor 13).

Golden Nugget

"THIS Knowledge nurtures us into the things of God."

Further Study

  • John 8:32
  • Joel 2:13
  • 1 Cor 13
  • Rev 3:15-16

The Mandate

Be a partaker of this truth. Desire to be transformed and wish the same for others, reaching out to the entire world to cause them to come to the knowledge of truth.
