The Revelation of Truth

15th March 2026

1 Pet. 1:22
"Since you have purified your souls in obeying the truth through the Spirit in sincere love of the brethren, love one another fervently with a pure heart." (NKJV)

Good morning! Yesterday, we explored the mandate of bringing all men to the knowledge of truth. But what is the truth? Truth is the substance of Jesus Christ in all existence.

As Christians, we must yearn for a revelation of the person of Christ in all things. This is the personal understanding in one's soul of who Christ is. When Peter replied, "You are the Christ, the Son of God," he was speaking from a place of unusual, divine understanding.

We must obey the truth we've received and ask for more revelation by putting a demand on the Spirit in our hearts. Seek the truth in Leviticus, seek it in the Song of Songs. After revealing His person in our hearts, we can manifest His awesomeness to the world.

Golden Nugget

"Today, commit yourself to seeking a revelation of the truth, which is Christ, the hope of glory."

Further Study

  • 1 Peter (General)
  • 2 Pet. 3:18
  • Mark 16:15
  • John 1:1-5

Prayer

Dear God, I understand there's more for me to know of Christ. Holy Spirit, help me see more of Christ's person as you stir a new hunger in me for more. Thank you, my Lord. Amen.
