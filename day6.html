Cast Away Religion: The Giving

Giving as an Overflow of the New Nature

Theme Scripture: 2 Corinthians 9:7
"So let each one give as he purposes in his heart, not grudgingly or of necessity; for God loves a cheerful giver." (NKJV)

Good morning. Yesterday we established that we are ingrafted in Him. Today, we look at the practical outworking of that union: The Giving.

The Difference: Religion teaches us to give so that we can "get" or "qualify" for a blessing. But the newness of life teaches us that we give because we have already received.

We no longer give out of fear or spiritual debt. We give because our Father, in whose likeness we were made, is a Giver. When your consciousness is aligned with the abundance of Christ, giving becomes a natural manifestation of who you are.

It is the finished work in action. We are citizens of heaven, the righteousness of God, and we live in an overflow that the world cannot explain. We give to replicate the heart of the One who gave everything at Golgotha.

Golden Nugget

"We do not give to be blessed; we give because we are the blessed of the Lord, manifesting His nature through our generosity."

Further Study

  • Luke 6:38
  • Acts 20:35
  • Proverbs 11:24-25

Prayer

Father, thank You for the abundance I carry in Christ. I thank You that I am a giver by nature, just as You are. Today, I yield to this new nature, giving with joy and without fear. Amen.
